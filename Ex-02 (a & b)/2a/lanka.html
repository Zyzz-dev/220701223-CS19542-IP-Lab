Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka, historically known as Ceylon, is an island country located off the southern coast of India. Here are some key facts about Sri Lanka:

  • Geography: It lies in the Indian Ocean, southwest of the Bay of Bengal, separated from the Indian peninsula by the Gulf of Mannar and the Palk Strait.
  • Languages: Sinhala, Tamil, and English are the major languages spoken.
  • Capital: The legislative capital is Sri Jayawardenepura Kotte, while the executive and judicial capital is Colombo.
  • History: Sri Lanka gained independence from Britain in 1948.
