Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu, often referred to as the “Land of Temples,” is the southernmost state of India. It boasts a rich cultural heritage, vibrant traditions, and a unique blend of history and modernity. Here are some key points about Tamil Nadu:

  • Geography and Area: Tamil Nadu covers an area of approximately 130,058 square kilometers, making it the tenth largest state in India.
  • Language and Culture: The state is home to the Tamil people, who speak Tamil, one of the oldest classical languages still in use.
  • Capital and Major Cities: The capital city is Chennai (formerly known as Madras), which is also the largest city.
  • Historical Significance: The state has a rich history, with influences from various dynasties, including the Cholas, Pandyas, and Pallavas.
